Top Rope - Papa Mango

Our first review of the month is one of our all time favourites from the lads over at Top Rop Brewing in Liverpool. It's a juicy, hazy mango pale ale, Papa Mango is dry hopped with Citra hops and then conditioned on 60kg of Alfonso mango puree. Think juicy mango overload with a soft hoppy finish. Remember Solero lolly ices when you were a child? think solero but in a glass, Yum!!!

Faith - Northern Monk

Next on the list is another big hit in the BryanRatesBeers office from our friends in Leeds. Faith by Northern monk. Faith is the brewers flag ship Ale and a must try for all new IPA drinkers. A Modern Hazy Pale Ale packed with soft fruit flavours. Fruity, refreshing and packs a little punch, perfect for a hot summers day

Pineapple Express - Tiny Rebel

To complete our Summer trio we take a jaunt over to sunny Wales to visit our friends at Tiny Rebel. It will come as no surprise to anyone what this next one tastes like. Its a smooth tangy Pineapple IPA. Not the faint hearted this IPA comes in at %6.2. Its so packed with flavour however its hard to noice as it glides down your gullet as tho it contains nothing but fruit juice!
